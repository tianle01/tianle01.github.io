About

I am a Year 3 student at the National University of Singapore, majoring in Engineering Science. People often ask what Engineering Science is. From my experience as an open house ambassador, I usually describe it as a broad engineering programme that spans mechanical, electrical, and software engineering, with a foundation in physics. It is sometimes described as a “jack of all trades, master of none”, but I think it does a good job of training students to see engineering systems at a higher level. As a result, many Engineering Science students go on to roles in project management, research, or similar paths taken by graduates from the core engineering disciplines. Roughly speaking, the curriculum feels like 30% mechanical, 30% electrical, 20% software, and 20% physics.
